Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 15, 2024

This Privacy Policy applies to the Seren Media Internal Marketing App (“the App”), owned and operated by Seren Media (“the Company”). The App is used to manage social media accounts on behalf of clients, such as Ocean Frontiers Dive Shop, with full permission to access and control their pages. This policy outlines how the App collects, uses, and protects data related to the management of these accounts.

1. Data Collection

The App collects data from third-party social media platforms, including Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, and other platforms where client accounts are managed. The data collected includes profile information, media, analytics, and content related to the management of client pages.

2. Data Use

Data collected by the App is used solely for managing social media pages on behalf of clients. This includes scheduling posts, retrieving social media analytics, and engaging with users. Seren Media does not use the data for any purpose other than managing the client’s accounts and fulfilling the terms of service agreements with its clients.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services: Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, and others as required for client account management. These platforms have their own privacy policies and terms of service, which govern the use of their data through the App. Seren Media accesses only the data necessary to manage client pages, in accordance with permissions granted by each platform.

4. Data Sharing

Data collected through the App is not shared with any third parties except for authorized personnel within Seren Media who are responsible for managing client accounts. No data is sold, rented, or used for marketing purposes outside the scope of client page management.

5. Security

Seren Media ensures that all data collected is stored securely and is only accessible by authorized personnel. Reasonable precautions are taken to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of client data.

6. Client Rights

Clients have the right to request information on how their data is managed and to revoke access to their social media accounts at any time. Any requests to delete, update, or modify data related to their accounts must be made through formal communication with Seren Media.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Seren Media reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy as necessary. Any updates will reflect changes in data handling practices and compliance with platform policies. Clients will be notified of significant changes.
